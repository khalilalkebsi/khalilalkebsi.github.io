About Me

I'm a Senior AI & Robotics Engineer with a Ph.D. in Control Theory & Engineering from East China University of Science and Technology (ECUST).

With over 8 years of experience in robotics, machine learning, and deep learning, I specialize in developing and deploying optimization algorithms for real-world industrial applications. My expertise spans control planning, autonomous systems, multi-robot coordination, and computer vision.

Currently at XPeng Motors, I lead the robotics initiatives within the Digitalization Manufacturing Process Department — focusing on manufacturing path planning, trajectory generation, motion control, and AI vision-guided robotic systems.
